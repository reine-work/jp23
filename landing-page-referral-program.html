JazzyPay’s Friend Referral Program

We’re always thinking of ways to get closer to our goal of empowering more businesses through cashless payments, and thus, our referral program began. Through this program, not only are we able to reach more businesses and help them upgrade their payment collection process, but we’re also able to empower our existing partners by providing valuable incentives!

Whether you’re a JazzyPay merchant or a payer, you will be given a cash reward for every successful business referral.

To learn how to refer a friend, please watch the video below:

If you’re a JazzyPay merchant, the cash reward will be deposited to your nominated corporate bank account. If you’re a payer, we will reach out to you so you can claim your cash reward. The specific amount you’ll receive will depend on the type of business you successfully refer.

For more information, please refer to our rewards chart below*:

Type of business
Referral reward
Hospitals ₱15,000 - ₱40,000
Schools ₱15,000 - ₱40,000
Clinics ₱500 - ₱2,000
Suppliers ₱1,000 - ₱3,000
Travel Agencies ₱1,000 - ₱5,000
Real Estate ₱1,000 - ₱5,000
Others ₱1,000 - ₱5,000
*This agreement contemplates an introduction only. The Referrer will receive a one-time referral fee in accordance with the Schedule of Fees mentioned, exclusive of the applicable sales tax, subject to withholding taxes. Referrer is only entitled to the commission in the event that the referred client shall formally engage with JazzyPay for the referred services/products within three (3) months from the date of actual referral. JazzyPay will send the payment to the designated bank account within thirty (30) days from the signing of the definitive agreement with the prospective client, the signing of the definitive agreement shall mean to include that the prospective client has been successfully onboarded in JazzyPay’s payment system.

Remember, a referral is only considered successful when your referred business’ account application gets approved, so make sure to remind your friends to provide all the required documents to complete their registration! As soon as your referred business gets approved, we will reach out to you to process your cash reward.
