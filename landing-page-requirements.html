Legal Requirements

Legal Requirements for Sole Proprietorship
  1. DTI Certificate of Business Registration
  2. Business Permit
  3. BIR 2303 Certificate of TAX Registration
  4. Two government ID of the proprietor
  5. Proof of billing address
  6. Signed Service Agreement
NOTE: We may also request additional requirements, certificates, or licenses depending on the type and nature of your business.

Legal Requirements for Partnership
  1. SEC Certificate of Partnership Registration
  2. Article of Partnership (AOP)
  3. Business Permit
  4. BIR 2303 Certificate of TAX Registration
  5. Secretary Certificate (Authorized Signatory)
  6. Two government IDs of each partner
  7. Signed Service Agreement
NOTE: We may also request additional requirements, certificates, or licenses depending on the type and nature of your business.

Legal Requirements for Corporation
  1. SEC Certificate of Corporate Registration
  2. Article of Incorporation (AOI)
  3. General Information Sheet (GIS)
  4. Business Permit
  5. BIR 2303 Certificate of TAX Registration
  6. Audited Financial Statement (AFS)
  7. Secretary Certificate (Authorized Signatory)
  8. Two government IDs of each Authorized Signatory
  9. Two government IDs of the Corporate Secretary
  10. Signed Service Agreement
NOTE: We may also request additional requirements, certificates, or licenses depending on the type and nature of your business.
