Why JazzyPay?

We do more for your business. Here’s how we support your growth and success.

Less fees, more growth

No setup fee, monthly subscription fee, or a monthly sales quota. Charges are only made for every successful transaction so you can focus on growing your business. See our pricing here.

Send invoices online

Streamline your payment collection and save time by scheduling payment requests and sending invoices individually or in bulk. Enable auto reminders to notify your clients of payment due dates.

Monitor payments in real time

Easily track payments and payment requests in real time through your profile dashboard.

Zero integration needed

Don’t have a website for your business? No worries, you can accept digital payments with JazzyPay even without a website.

Top notch security

JazzyPay is a registered Operator of Payments System by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas. We comply with the highest security standards to ensure that you and your clients’ information are always secure.

How it works

Scan QR / Search

Enter the name of the company you’d like to pay or Scan their QR Code.

Select payment method

Enter payment details and choose your payment option from credit or debit cards, online bank payments, e-wallets, or over-the-counter cash deposits.

Review and pay
